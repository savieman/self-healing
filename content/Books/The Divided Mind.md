# The Divided Mind

## Description

<p>In his ground-breaking and perennially popular book that helped change the way we think about our health, Dr Sarno charts the spectrum and history of psychosomatic disorders – which can include chronic pain, carpal tunnel, IBS and migraine – and describes their psychology, including how knowledge and awareness can have a restorative impact.
